01 · GETTING STARTED
HOW TO READ YOUR CHARTS
Every chart in this guide is a 13×13 grid of all 169 starting hands. Learn to read one, and you can read every
chart in this guide.
ANATOMY OF A CHART KNOW YOUR POSITION
POCKET PAIRS Run down the diagonal, from AA to 22. UTG Under The Gun CO Cutoff
UTG+1 Under The Gun +1 BTN Button
SUITED Fill the upper-right triangle: AKs, QJs, T9s, etc.
LJ Lojack SB Small Blind
OFFSUIT Fill the lower-left triangle: AKo, QJo, T9o, etc.
HJ Hijack BB Big Blind
WHAT YOU'LL FIND INSIDE · FOUR SITUATIONS
RFI (RAISE FIRST IN) which hands to open when everyone folds before you.
VS RFI how to respond when someone raises before you.
VS 3-BET how to continue after you open and face a 3-bet.
VS 4-BET how to continue after you 3-bet and face a 4-bet.
These charts are an excellent default starting point, and you only need to deviate from them when you know how
to exploit your opponents based on their mistakes.
